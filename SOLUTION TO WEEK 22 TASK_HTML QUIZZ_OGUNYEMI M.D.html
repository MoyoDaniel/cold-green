Brief History About Dufuna Canoe:
Dufuna Canoe is a canoe discobered in 1987 by a yoruba fulani cattle herdsman a few kilometers form the village of Dufuna in the Fune Local Gov. Area, not far from the Komadugu Gana River, in Yobe State, Nigeria. Raidocarbon dating of a sample of charcoal found near the site dates the canoe at 8500 to 8000 years old, linking the site to Lake Mega Chad. It is the oldest boat to be discovered in Africa, and the second oldest konwn worldwide. The canoe is currently in Damaturu,the state capital. For more information about Dufuna Canoe click here.
